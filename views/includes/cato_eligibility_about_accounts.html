Do you need to report these things in your accounts?

You can’t file to HM Revenue and Customs using this service if you need to include the following things in your accounts:

  • leased cars
  • assets you're claiming as capital allowances in your special or single asset pools
  • ancillary or non-trading income over £1,000 - income from activities that aren't related to your main trade
  • property income over £5,200 (gross), or a loss on a property

    • You can’t file to HM Revenue and Customs with this service

    You can still use this service to file with Companies House. You can file separately with HM Revenue and Customs, or you can use accounting software.

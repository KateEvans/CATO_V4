HMRC period dates

Your HMRC reporting period start and end on the same dates as your Companies House reporting period. But if your Companies House reporting period is longer than 12 months, you'll need 2 HMRC periods.

HMRC reporting periods can be a maximum of 12 months. So if your Companies House period is longer than 12 months, you'll need an extra HMRC period to fill the gap.

HMRC period 1

Your first HMRC period has to start on the same date as your Companies House period. It can be a maximum of 12 months.

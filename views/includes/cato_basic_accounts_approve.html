HMRC require submission of 'full' micro-entity accounts i.e. Directors Report, Profit & Loss Account, Balance Sheet, and Footnotes (if provided).

However, Companies House will accept 'basic' micro-entity accounts i.e. Balance Sheet and Footnotes (if provided).

These 'basic' Micro-entity Accounts are extracted from the 'full' Micro-entity Accounts that you have already entered.

The following pages are copied exactly into the Micro-entity Accounts:

  • About the company
  • Balance sheet
  • Footnotes (if provided)

In addition, the Balance Sheet will contain a statement that the accounts have been prepared and delivered in accordance with the provisions of the small companies regime applicable to micro-entities.

If you need to change any of the figures or text in these pages you can go back to the appropriate page in the Micro-entity Accounts. You will have to approve the revised version.

Companies House recommend that you review and print the Accounts before you submit them, to be sure that the appropriate information will be placed on the public record.

Continue to HM Revenue and Custom's new filing software to file your return.

You need to use HMRC's old PDF filing software instead if you’re amending a previously filed return. You also need to use the PDF software if your organisation is a:

  • charity with income below £6.5 million
  • community amateur sports club (CASC)
  • members' club
  • company in liquidation
  • company limited by guarantee

You’ll need to use commercial software instead of the HMRC software options if any of the following apply:

  • your turnover is more than £6.5 million
  • the period you want to file with HMRC for is covered by more than one set of statutory accounts
  • your accounts cover more than 3 HMRC Corporation Tax periods
  • you’re reporting a repayment of money owed to the company (eg a director’s loan) that happened more than 9 months after the end of the period you're filing for

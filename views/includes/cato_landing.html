You can use this service to file your company accounts to Companies House, and your accounts and tax return (CT600) to HM Revenue and Customs.

This service is for small limited companies with simple finances. Accountants should not use this service for their clients.

Find out more about company accounts and tax returns.

Before you start

You’ll need your finalised and balanced company accounts before you file them with this service. Get sample accounts and templates.

You’ll need these things to hand:

  • your company registration number
  • your authentication code from Companies House (this was sent to you by post - request a new code if you need to)
  • your HMRC Government Gateway login details

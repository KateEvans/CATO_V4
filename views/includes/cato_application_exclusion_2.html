You’ll be able to use this service in the next few months if your organisation is a:

  • charity with income below £6.5 million
  • community amateur sports club (CASC)
  • company in liquidation

You can use HM Revenue and Customs’ PDF filing software to file your tax return and accounts until then.

You can’t use this service to file your company accounts and tax return. You can file separately with Companies House, or you can use accounting software.

Other filing options

You can also use tax software to file your tax return to HMRC.

And you can file your accounts on the Companies House website, or use accounting software.
